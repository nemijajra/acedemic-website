About Me

I am a research scholar in biomedical physics with research interests in fluorescence spectroscopy, optical diagnostics, and biomedical instrumentation. My work involves the development and optimization of optical devices for early detection of oral and cervical cancer, with an emphasis on clinically translatable and cost-effective solutions.

Research Interests

  • Biomedical Optics
  • Fluorescence Spectroscopy
  • Medical Imaging Systems
  • Signal & Data Analysis
